**Type:** Read-only visual / product audit  
**Verdict:** NEEDS ATTENTION  
**Date:** May 2026

## Objective

Audit whether users will naturally discover Reflection once it exists — **visual hierarchy and discoverability only** (not logic).

---

## Core finding

80J-B placement **below** the collapsed card read as **floating helper text**, not part of “This month.” Calm and architecturally safe, but **too low** for Stability Room discoverability.

| Dimension | Assessment |
|-----------|------------|
| Visibility | Moderate in-section; weak page-level (bottom, post-save) |
| Hierarchy | Tertiary footnote; competed with Edit |
| Discoverability risk | Medium–high first use; **high** repeat / mobile / scroll |
| Beynd alignment | Calm ✓ · Premium / reflective **underdeveloped** |

---

## Relationship to “This month”

Users perceived **B leaning toward C** — associated but outside the card border → footnote, not card action.

---

## Preferred placement

**Option B — inside collapsed card footer** (within bordered card, below summary, subtle divider).

Ranked: B > C > A (current) > D > E.

---

## Recommendation

**NEEDS ATTENTION** — refine hierarchy before 80K.
